Overview

9 Degrees Alexandria is an incredible climbing experience, with challenging climbs that vary widely. The first 9 Degrees Gym, and certainly one of the best ones.

Pros:

  • A wide variety of different climbs
  • Frequent rotations in climbs
  • They also sell pizza

Cons:

  • Climbs are more challenging than other gyms
  • Almost too much variation between climbs
  • No bubbler
