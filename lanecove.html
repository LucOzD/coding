Overview

9 Degrees Lane Cove has a great community for more frequent climbers. It offers medium difficulty climbs that are challenging to a fun extent. A great place to frequent with friends or alone.

Pros:

  • Very Friendly Staff
  • Medium Dificulty Climbs
  • Two dynamic walls

Cons:

  • Smaller than the others
  • Very hidden location
  • Not as big as the others
