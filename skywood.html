Overview

Skywood Bouldering offers a wide range of walls to choose from, and is a short walk from Manly Beach. A great location to take friends and family to.

Pros:

  • Beginner Friendly
  • Great Atmosphere
  • Lots of food nearby

Cons:

  • Hard to find parkng spots in Manly
  • Resets less
  • Less variety in climbs
