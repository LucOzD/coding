Overview

9 Degrees Waterloo is an amazing bouldering gym; with giant facilities, friendly staff and even a shoe repair shop. A great place to go alone or with friends, with a variety of different styles of climbs.

Pros:

  • Friendly Staff
  • Large Facilities
  • Wide range of Difficulties

Cons:

  • Large Crowds
  • Routes take time to Reset
  • Tends to be Loud
